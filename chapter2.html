The Midnight Vow
Chapter Two

Fellow mates

My real name isn't Bruce, but as far as I remember, that's what everyone called me. Some part of me says that's not what my name was, but it doesn't matter; it just adds a layer above my masks.

My mom and dad died when I was 3. They say car accident, but I don't believe it. After they died, my uncle took care of me, but no one can handle you forever. So, I was sent to boarding school when I was 6. That pretty much explains the way I am.

"Read page number 25, line number 30... Bruce."

I stood up and started reading.

"His hands were full of blood, disappointment in his eyes. He feared what he had done, but there was no going back. Horror on his face... was this his destiny?"

"Sit down, you expressed the emotions too well."

My class was full of people who pissed me off. Some are too overfriendly but still talk behind each other's backs. Others are quiet but clear visionaries. Some are polite but hide the irritation they have. Some are confident but still cry in the night... I always wondered, what kind of person am I? But no answer came from inside.
